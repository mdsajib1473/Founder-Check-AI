# FounderCheck - AI-Powered Startup Validator for Bangladesh

## 🎯 Product Overview

**FounderCheck** is an AI-powered startup validation platform that helps entrepreneurs in Bangladesh validate their business ideas in **60 seconds** using data-driven analysis across 11 comprehensive modules.

---

## 🚀 What's Currently Built (Phase 1)

### Core Features (✅ Complete)
1. **Market Demand Analysis**
   - TAM/SAM/SOM estimation
   - Competitive landscape
   - Customer pain points
   - Market score (1-10)

2. **Regulatory Risk Assessment**
   - Bangladesh compliance checklist
   - Key regulators identification
   - Timeline to approval
   - Cost estimation

3. **Business Model Canvas**
   - Visual 9-box SVG diagram
   - Key partners, activities, resources
   - Revenue streams & cost structure
   - Value proposition highlighting

4. **Competitive Analysis**
   - Direct competitor ranking (with %)
   - Market share distribution
   - Estimated revenue & users
   - Strengths & weaknesses
   - Indirect competitors analysis
   - Market trends
   - Market gaps identification

5. **Bangladesh Market Impact**
   - Local regulations
   - Market potential in BDT
   - Cultural factors
   - Economic opportunities
   - Supply chain insights
   - Localization recommendations

6. **SWOT Analysis**
   - 2x2 matrix visualization
   - Color-coded quadrants
   - Strength/weakness assessment
   - Opportunity identification
   - Threat analysis

7. **Go-to-Market Strategy**
   - 3-phase timeline (Launch → Growth → Scale)
   - Customer acquisition channels
   - Pricing strategy
   - Partnership targets
   - Visual timeline

8. **Risk Assessment**
   - Probability vs impact heat map
   - High/medium priority risks
   - Mitigation strategies
   - Overall risk score
   - Severity indicators

9. **Founder-Market Fit**
   - Fit score (1-10) with gauge
   - Required skills assessment
   - Experience gaps
   - Team recommendations
   - Improvement roadmap

10. **Investor Q&A Interview**
    - 10 tough investor questions
    - Real-time scoring
    - Feedback on answers
    - Practice mode
    - Final score calculation

11. **Analysis History**
    - Track all analyses
    - Readiness score history
    - Q&A completion status
    - One-click reload previous analyses
